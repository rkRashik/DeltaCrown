COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

The DeltaCrown
Community Code

DeltaCrown is Bangladesh's competitive esports home. These guidelines protect the space where thousands of players, teams, and fans come to compete, share, and grow.

Allowed Prohibited Caution
1
Respect every player

Disagree on strategy, argue map picks, debate meta — that's the culture. But personal attacks, slurs, targeted harassment, or sustained bullying against any player, team, or community member are not tolerated.

Trash talk (friendly) Gameplay criticism Slurs Doxxing Sustained harassment
2
Post content relevant to esports

Share match highlights, clips, strategies, team news, LFT posts, tournament updates, and discussion about the games we host. Off-topic spam, unrelated advertising, and mass link-dumps will be removed.

Clips & highlights Strategy guides LFT / recruitment Unrelated spam Promotional posts (max 1/day)
3
No cheating, match-fixing, or account fraud

Discussing known exploits for educational purposes is fine. Posting instructions to use hacks, advertising boosting services, sharing match-fixing signals, or coordinating account sharing violates this rule and may result in permanent ban and tournament disqualification.

Exploit discussion (educational) Hack distribution Boosting ads Match-fixing signals
4
No misinformation about results or standings

Fabricating match scores, falsely claiming tournament victories, or spreading misinformation about other teams' standings damages competitive integrity. Always link to official match reports when referencing results.

Sharing official results Fabricated scores False standings claims
5
Language & content standards

Bangla and English posts are equally welcome. Profanity is tolerated in the spirit of competitive culture but heavy use in targeted attacks is not. NSFW media, graphic violence, or content harmful to minors is strictly prohibited at all times.

Bangla / English Light profanity NSFW Graphic violence
6
Team identity & impersonation

Only verified captains and co-leaders may post on behalf of a team. Using a team's name or logo without authorization, or impersonating staff or other players, will result in immediate post removal and potential suspension.

Team post (captain/co-leader) Impersonation Unauthorized use of team identity
7
Respect staff decisions

DeltaCrown staff moderate with fairness. If you believe a moderation action was incorrect, use the support channel — do not post appeals publicly or harass staff members.

Enforcement
First offence
Warning + post removal
Repeat offence
Temporary post restriction (1–7 days)
Severe / repeat
Permanent community ban + tournament review

Staff may act immediately without warning for serious violations (doxxing, NSFW, match-fixing, or illegal content).
