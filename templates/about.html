What DeltaCrown is

A connected esports ecosystem, not just an event page.

DeltaCrown starts from Bangladesh and South Asia, where serious players and teams often grow through scattered chats, manual brackets, informal payment handling, and fragile proof flows. The platform direction is to give those communities a more trusted path: structured competition, visible identity, team operations, Crown Points progression, and DeltaCoin utility inside the platform.

The product is active and still evolving. Several systems already exist in code, while deeper automation, richer dispute experiences, game API verification, and advanced training workflows are intentionally treated as next steps instead of being presented as finished promises.

Ecosystem spine

The core systems that hold the platform together.

Tournaments

Teams & Organizations

Persistent team identities, rosters, roles, public pages, recruitment, sponsors, media, and organization structures.

Game Passport

Player identity for game accounts, preferred titles, public profile context, and future verification direction.

Crown Points

The public ranking language for competitive progression, team tiers, activity, history, and seasonal direction.

DeltaCoin

A closed-loop platform utility and reward currency used inside DeltaCrown for entries, rewards, wallet records, and platform services.

Team HQ

Team operations for scrims, tryouts, practice sessions, VOD reviews, applications, offers, and roster workflows.

Proof & Review

Result proof, Mission proof, uploaded evidence, dispute notes, protected media routes, and staff review queues.

Operator Console

Admin-only surfaces that guide Mission creation, review queues, Dropzone setup, and service-backed competitive actions.

Competitive modes

More than tournaments, with clear names and review-backed flows.

Showdown

Head-to-head competitive reward matches using team authority, Match Room handoff, result handling, and admin controls.

Missions

Solo skill goals and reward tasks with URL/image proof submission, review states, and operator completion controls.

Bounty

Open skill-based challenges and claim review workflows with admin verification and dispute-aware handling.

Dropzone

Battle royale custom lobby workflows for entries, room credentials, scoring, settlement, and operator review.

Integrity layer

Competition needs trust, not just hype.

DeltaCrown treats competitive integrity as product infrastructure: account standards, team authority, proof capture, review queues, protected evidence access, dispute records, moderation sanctions, and operator-controlled settlement paths. The goal is not to promise perfect automation. The goal is to keep sensitive decisions inside traceable review surfaces instead of scattered messages.
