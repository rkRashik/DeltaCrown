FAQs

How do payments work right now?
We verify payments manually (bKash/Nagad/Rocket). Entry fees and prize payouts are tracked in an immutable ledger.
What happens if there’s a match dispute?
Submit evidence; referees rule using standardized criteria. All actions are logged for transparency.
Is the platform mobile friendly?
Yes. We design for mobile first and support low-bandwidth scenarios.
