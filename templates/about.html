Our Origin Story

Why DeltaCrown Exists

Founded by Redwanul Rashik in 2025, DeltaCrown was born from a simple observation: esports infrastructure is broken.

The Problem

  • • Tournaments disappear after they end
  • • Teams lack long-term identity
  • • Players grind endlessly without recognition
  • • Emerging regions lack accessible systems
  • • Fragmented tools across multiple platforms

Our Solution

  • • Persistent competitive history
  • • Professional team organizations
  • • Verified career progression
  • • Local payment support (bKash, Nagad, Rocket)
  • • Unified ecosystem for everything esports

Our Core Belief

Talent is universal, opportunity isn't. We're building the infrastructure to change that—turning scattered potential into verified careers, and isolated matches into a sustainable esports future.

Who We Are

What Is DeltaCrown?

Vision

Building a world where geography does not define esports destiny. From Bangladesh to South Asia and beyond—creating trusted pathways where every player, regardless of location, has a fair shot at competitive glory.

Mission

Make esports a respected profession by providing trusted tournaments, verified teams, and credible pathways. We empower players to build careers, not just compete in isolated events—from their first match to their legacy.

The Engine

How It Works: The Tournament Lifecycle

Tournaments are the engine that drives everything—from smart registration to verified results, every match becomes a credible competitive record.

1

Creation & Configuration

2

3

4

Verification & Disputes

Evidence-based verification, structured dispute filing, referee intervention, complete audit trails.

Rankings & Rewards
