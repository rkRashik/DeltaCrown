Privacy Policy

Your Data Rights and Our Commitments

Effective Date: December 16, 2025
• Last Modified: December 16, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

At DeltaCrown, we are committed to protecting your personal information and maintaining transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. This Privacy Policy explains our practices and your rights under Bangladesh data protection laws and international standards.

By using DeltaCrown, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username (display name)
  • Date of birth
  • Country/region
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile photo (optional)

1.2 Tournament and Gaming Data

During platform usage, we automatically collect:

  • Tournament registrations and participation history
  • Match results, scores, and performance statistics
  • Team affiliations, roles, and roster changes
  • Leaderboard rankings and rating calculations
  • In-game identifiers (IGN, UID, player IDs)
  • DeltaCoin balance, transactions, and earning history
  • Achievement unlocks and milestone progress

1.3 Payment and Transaction Data

For tournament fees and prize distribution:

  • bKash/Nagad/Rocket account numbers
  • Bank account details (for prize claims)
  • Transaction IDs and payment receipts
  • Payment history and billing records
  • Identity verification documents (NID, passport for prize claims)

Note: We do not store complete credit card numbers or CVV codes. Payment processing is handled by secure third-party providers (bKash, Nagad, Rocket).

1.4 Device and Usage Data

We automatically collect technical information:

Device Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)
  • Screen resolution

Usage Analytics

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Click patterns and navigation paths
  • Referral sources
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Error logs and crash reports

1.5 Communications and User Content

Content you share on the platform:

  • Support ticket messages and inquiries
  • Team chat messages and communications
  • Dispute reports and moderation submissions
  • Forum posts and community interactions
  • Uploaded media (team logos, certificates, payment proofs)

  • • Tournament registration and bracket management

  • • Leaderboard ranking calculations
  • • Prize distribution processing

Communications

  • • Tournament updates and reminders
  • • Account security alerts

Analytics & Improvement

  • • Platform performance optimization
  • • User experience enhancement
  • • Feature usage analysis
  • • Bug identification and fixing
  • • Trend analysis and reporting

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

Trusted third parties who assist in platform operations:

  • Payment Processors: bKash, Nagad, Rocket for transaction processing
  • Cloud Hosting: Server infrastructure and data storage providers
  • Email Services: Transactional email delivery systems
  • Analytics Tools: Google Analytics for usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Security Services: DDoS protection and anti-fraud systems

3.2 Tournament Organizers and Partners

When you participate in tournaments, we may share your username, in-game ID, team affiliation, and match statistics with tournament organizers, sponsors, and broadcasting partners for event management and promotional purposes.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information to comply with:

  • Legal obligations under Bangladesh law (Digital Security Act 2018)
  • Court orders, subpoenas, or legal process
  • Law enforcement requests and investigations
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevention of fraud or illegal activities

Public Information

Your username, profile photo, team affiliations, tournament history, and leaderboard rankings are publicly visible on the platform. In-game statistics and match results may be displayed publicly for competitive integrity and transparency.

4. Your Privacy Rights

Under Bangladesh data protection laws and international standards, you have the following rights:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you, including account details, transaction history, and activity logs.

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or outdated personal information in your account settings or by contacting support.

Right to Deletion

Request permanent deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

Right to Data Portability

Receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON/CSV) for transfer to another service.

Right to Object

Opt out of marketing communications, analytics tracking, or other non-essential data processing activities.

Right to Restrict Processing

Temporarily suspend processing of your data while disputing accuracy or contesting our legal basis for processing.

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at deltacrownhq@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days and may request identity verification for security purposes.

Note: Some data may be retained for legal compliance (tax records, fraud prevention) even after account deletion.

5. Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

Encryption

SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission. Passwords hashed using bcrypt.

Access Controls

Role-based permissions. Two-factor authentication available for accounts.

Monitoring

24/7 security monitoring. Regular security audits and penetration testing.

Data Breach Protocol

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify affected users within 72 hours via email and platform notifications, as required by Bangladesh Digital Security Act 2018. We will provide details about the breach, affected data, and remediation steps.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations:

Data Type Retention Period
Account information Until account deletion + 30 days
Tournament/match data 3 years for integrity records
Payment/tax records 7 years (legal requirement)
Cheat/ban records Permanent (platform integrity)
Analytics data Anonymized after 18 months

7. International Data Transfers

DeltaCrown is based in Bangladesh, but our services utilize international infrastructure. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Bangladesh, including:

  • • Cloud hosting providers in USA, Singapore, Europe
  • • CDN services for global content delivery
  • • Analytics platforms for usage monitoring

We ensure adequate safeguards through standard contractual clauses, data processing agreements, and selecting providers with robust security certifications (ISO 27001, SOC 2).

8. Children's Privacy

Users must be at least 13 years old to create an account. For users aged 13-17, we require parental or guardian consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without proper consent, we will delete that information immediately. Parents or guardians can contact us at deltacrownhq@gmail.com to review, modify, or delete their child's information.

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

DeltaCrown uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze platform usage, and maintain security. For detailed information about our cookie practices, please see our Cookie Policy.

Cookie Categories

  • Functional: Preferences, language settings (optional)
  • Analytics: Usage statistics, performance monitoring (optional)
  • Advertising: Currently not used on DeltaCrown
