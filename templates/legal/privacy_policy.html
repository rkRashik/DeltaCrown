Data transparency

Privacy Policy

This policy explains the information DeltaCrown collects and uses to operate accounts, profiles, teams, tournaments, competitive modes, wallets, proof review, moderation, support, and platform security.

Effective date: May 18, 2026

Data we collect How we use it Visibility Sharing Retention Security Choices

Plain-language summary

DeltaCrown collects the data needed to run an esports platform: accounts, profiles, team membership, tournament entries, Match Room activity, wallet records, proof uploads, disputes, moderation, support messages, and technical logs. We do not claim third-party certifications or legal compliance programs that have not been verified in the codebase.

1. Data we collect

Account data

Username, email, password credentials handled by authentication systems, account status, login/session records, and settings.

Profile and Game Passport data

Display identity, bios, avatars, linked game identities, preferred games, achievements, certificates, social links, privacy controls, and public profile content.

Team and organization data

Team names, tags, rosters, roles, applications, tryouts, scrims, practice sessions, VOD reviews, public team media, sponsors, and organization relationships.

Tournament and competitive data

Registrations, eligibility, schedules, Match Rooms, results, scores, standings, Crown Points, Showdown, Missions, Bounty, Dropzone entries, claims, and scoring records.

Wallet and DeltaCoin records

Wallet balances, transaction history, entry fees, escrow records, rewards, refunds, top-up/withdrawal requests, payment method fields, and wallet security records.

Proof, dispute, and moderation data

Uploaded proof files, proof URLs, screenshots, match media, dispute evidence, dispute notes, abuse reports, moderation sanctions, and review decisions.

Support data

Contact form name, email, subject, message, account link if logged in, IP address, user agent, status, priority, and internal admin notes.

Technical data

Session cookies, CSRF tokens, device/browser information, IP address, request logs, security logs, and optional analytics where enabled in production configuration.

2. How we use data

  • To create accounts, authenticate users, keep sessions secure, and apply user preferences.
  • To operate profiles, Game Passports, teams, organizations, tournaments, Match Rooms, Team HQ, and competitive modes.
  • To calculate rankings, Crown Points, match records, team activity, and competitive history.
  • To maintain wallet records, DeltaCoin transactions, entry fees, refunds, escrow, rewards, and settlement review.
  • To review proof, disputes, abuse reports, moderation issues, support requests, and operator queues.
  • To prevent cheating, fraud, spam, account abuse, wallet abuse, and unsafe conduct.
  • To diagnose errors, improve platform performance, and understand aggregate product usage.

3. Public and internal visibility

Some information is public by design, such as public profiles, team pages, rosters, tournament standings, match results, Crown Points, achievements, and public team media. Other information is limited to the account owner, team roles, related participants, staff, or operators depending on the feature.

Proof files, dispute evidence, Match Room media, wallet records, support messages, and moderation records may be restricted and reviewed by authorized staff or related participants where the product flow allows it.

4. Sharing and service providers

DeltaCrown may use infrastructure, hosting, storage, database, email, analytics, payment, security, and support providers to operate the platform. We may also disclose information when required to comply with valid legal requests, enforce our rules, protect users, investigate abuse, or secure the platform.

5. Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, preserve competitive records, maintain wallet and ledger history, resolve disputes, handle moderation, support legal or operational needs, and protect platform integrity. Some records may remain after account changes because tournaments, teams, rankings, wallet ledgers, proofs, and moderation histories can affect other users or platform integrity.

6. Security

DeltaCrown uses platform controls such as authentication, CSRF protection, permission checks, role checks, admin access controls, protected proof routes, and review workflows. No online service can guarantee perfect security. If you believe your account or data is at risk, contact support promptly.

7. Your choices

  • You can update many account, profile, Game Passport, social, and privacy settings in the product where available.
  • You can request help with privacy, security, or account questions through support.
  • You can control cookies through your browser. See the Cookie Policy for more detail.

8. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent through DeltaCrown Support or by email at deltacrownhq@gmail.com.
