Cookie Policy

This page explains the cookies and similar browser storage DeltaCrown uses to keep the platform logged in, secure, functional, and measurable.

Effective date: May 18, 2026

Short version

DeltaCrown uses essential cookies for authentication and security, may use preference storage for interface settings where implemented, and may load analytics in production configuration. You can control cookies from your browser settings.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small pieces of data stored by your browser. Similar technologies such as local storage may also remember interface settings or help product features work properly.

2. Cookies DeltaCrown uses

Type Examples Purpose
Session and authentication sessionid Keeps you logged in and connects requests to your account session.
Security csrftoken Helps protect forms and state-changing requests from cross-site request forgery.
Preferences browser storage where enabled May remember interface choices such as theme, dismissed UI, dashboard state, or product settings where implemented.
Analytics production analytics scripts where configured May help understand aggregate usage, page performance, and product improvements. Analytics availability depends on production configuration.

3. Why essential cookies are required

Authentication, session, and CSRF cookies are required for login, forms, account actions, tournament registration, Team HQ, wallet flows, proof submission, contact forms, and admin/operator access. Disabling them may prevent core platform features from working.

4. Managing cookies

You can block, delete, or limit cookies through your browser settings. If you block essential cookies, DeltaCrown may not be able to keep you logged in or process secure requests.

DeltaCrown does not currently claim a full in-app cookie preference center. If that changes, this policy should be updated with the active controls.

5. Contact

Questions about cookies can be sent through DeltaCrown Support or by email at deltacrownhq@gmail.com.
