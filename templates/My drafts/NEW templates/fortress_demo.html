Platform Economy Dictionary

Internal reference guide for definitions, metrics, and compliance policies.

Master Treasury Root Node

The ultimate source of all DeltaCoins. It starts at a `0` balance. When new DC is minted or given to users, the Master Treasury's balance decreases (goes negative). This ensures the Double-Entry Ledger remains balanced at exactly 0.

Formula: Sum(All User Wallets) + Treasury Balance = 0

Market Circulation

The total absolute number of DeltaCoins currently held by all users, organizations, and escrows across the entire platform. This number should always be the exact positive inverse of the Master Treasury deficit.

Condition: Circulation == |Master Treasury|

Total User Inflow

The volume of DC that entered circulation because users purchased it with real fiat money (bKash/Nagad top-ups). This represents the "Fiat-Backed" portion of the economy and actual company revenue.

Admin Outflow

The volume of DC injected into circulation for free via automated rewards (Welcome, KYC), tournament platform payouts, or manual admin airdrops. This represents "Promotional" or "Platform Cost" DC.
