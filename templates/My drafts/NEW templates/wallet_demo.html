Platform Economy Principles

Understanding how DeltaCrown maintains a safe, compliant ecosystem.

Closed-Loop Utility Token

DeltaCoins (DC) are strictly digital platform utility assets used for tournament entries and challenges. DC cannot be withdrawn, refunded, or converted back to fiat currency under any circumstances.

Fiat Prize Separation

Official tournament prize pools listed in BDT (Fiat) are disbursed entirely separate from the DeltaCoin economy. Winners must claim fiat prizes through the Prize Claims portal using local MFS channels.

Escrow Integrity

When engaging in challenges or bounties, DC is temporarily held in a neutral smart-escrow. Funds are released automatically to the victor upon verified match completion, ensuring 100% fair play.
