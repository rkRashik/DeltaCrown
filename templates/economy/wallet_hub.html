What is DeltaCoin (DC)?

DeltaCoin (DC) is the official, secure competitive asset of the DeltaCrown ecosystem. It is a closed-loop utility token designed exclusively to fuel your esports journey. You use DC to enter premium tournaments, place wagers in challenges, and claim bounties.

Important: DC is not cryptocurrency and holds no real-world fiat value. It cannot be withdrawn, cashed out, or exchanged back into BDT.

Core Economic Principles

Fiat Prize Separation

Official tournament prize pools advertised in BDT (Fiat) are completely separate from the DeltaCoin system. If you win an official tournament, you will generate a "Prize Claim" ticket. Admins will verify your identity (KYC) and disburse the real cash directly to your Mobile Financial Service (MFS) like bKash or Nagad.

The Escrow Engine

When engaging in 1v1 challenges or bounties, both players' DC is temporarily deducted and held in a neutral "Escrow" state. Funds are only released automatically to the victor upon verified match completion. If a match is cancelled, the DC is instantly refunded.

Wager Limits & Integrity

To prevent fraud in non-API (manual screenshot) games, DeltaCrown enforces a strict maximum wager limit of 1,000 DC per challenge. Games with direct API integrations (like Valorant) have higher limits due to automated result verification.
