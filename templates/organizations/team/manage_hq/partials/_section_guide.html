Competitive Rules

Tournament Eligibility

  • Team must have the minimum number of active players for the tournament format
  • All players must have a completed Game Passport for the team's game
  • Team must be in ACTIVE status (not suspended or disbanded)
  • Entry fee (if any) must be paid before registration closes
  • Roster changes are locked once tournament check-in begins

Fair Play & Conduct

  • No smurfing or account sharing during competitive play
  • Match results must be reported within the allotted time window
  • Disputes are handled by platform moderators — no retaliation
  • Cheating, exploits, or toxic behavior leads to team suspension
  • Prize payouts follow the tournament payout schedule

Team Management Handbook

How do I invite a player?
Go to Roster Ops and click Invite Player. Enter their username or email. They'll receive a notification and have 7 days to accept. You can also share an invite link from the same modal.
How do I change my team name?
Go to Team Profile → Team Information. Name changes have a 30-day cooldown. If your team is in an active tournament, name and tag changes are blocked. For urgent changes, contact support.
How do I register for a tournament?
Go to Competition Hub and browse available tournaments. Click Register, select your lineup, and confirm. If there's an entry fee, it will be charged from your Treasury wallet.
What is a Game Passport?
A Game Passport is your player profile for a specific game. It includes your in-game name (IGN), rank, platform, and primary role. Each team member should have a Game Passport for the team's game. Managers can check player passport status from the roster cards.
How do I transfer or disband the team?
Only the Owner can transfer or disband a team. Go to Settings → Danger Zone. Transferring ownership will make you a Manager. Disbanding is irreversible and will remove all members. Teams in active tournaments cannot be disbanded.
