Handbook — Common Questions

How do I invite a player?
Go to Roster Ops → Invite Member. Enter their username or email. They receive a notification and have 7 days to accept. You can also share an invite link from the same modal.
How do I change my team name?
Go to Team Profile → Team Information. Name changes have a 30-day cooldown. If your team is in an active tournament, name and tag changes are blocked. Contact support for urgent changes.
How do I register for a tournament?
Go to Competitive and browse available tournaments. Click Register, select your lineup, and confirm. Entry fees are deducted from your Treasury wallet.
What is a Game Passport?
A Game Passport links your in-game account (IGN, rank, platform, region) to your DeltaCrown profile for a specific game. Every player competing in tournaments needs one. Managers can check passport status from roster cards.
How do I transfer or disband the team?
Only the Owner can transfer or disband. Go to Settings → Danger Zone. Disbanding is irreversible — all members are removed. Teams in active tournaments cannot be disbanded.
