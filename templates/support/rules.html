1. Eligibility Requirements

General Eligibility

  • Players must be at least 13 years old to participate in DeltaCrown tournaments
  • Players under 18 must have parental consent to participate
  • All players must have a valid DeltaCrown account in good standing
  • Players must not be subject to any active bans or suspensions
  • Participants agree to provide accurate information during registration

Regional Restrictions

While DeltaCrown primarily serves Bangladesh and emerging markets, certain tournaments may have regional restrictions based on:

  • Game server availability and latency requirements
  • Legal compliance with local gaming regulations
  • Payment method availability for prize distribution
  • Age verification requirements in specific jurisdictions

5. Anti-Cheat Policy

Zero Tolerance Policy

DeltaCrown maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy toward cheating, hacking, or any form of unfair advantage. We employ advanced detection systems and manual review processes to ensure competitive integrity.

Prohibited Activities

Cheating Software:

Using aimbots, wallhacks, ESPs, triggerb ots, or any unauthorized third-party software that provides competitive advantages.

Match Fixing:

Deliberately losing, colluding with opponents, or manipulating match outcomes for personal gain or to influence betting.

Account Sharing:

Allowing another player to compete using your account or using someone else's account during tournaments.

Smurfing:

Using secondary accounts to compete below your skill level or evade bans and restrictions.

Exploits & Bugs:

Intentionally exploiting game bugs, glitches, or map exploits to gain unfair advantages.

Reporting Suspicious Activity

If you suspect cheating or rule violations:

  1. 1. Collect evidence (screenshots, video recordings, match IDs)
  2. 2. Submit a report through the support system or email deltacrownhq@gmail.com
  3. 3. Do not publicly accuse players without conclusive evidence
  4. 4. Allow our moderation team to investigate and take appropriate action

All reports are treated confidentially. False accusations made in bad faith may result in penalties.

6. Dispute Resolution

Filing a Dispute

Disputes must be filed within 30 minutes of match completion. Include:

  • Match ID and tournament details
  • Clear description of the issue
  • Supporting evidence (screenshots, videos, timestamps)
  • Contact information for follow-up

Resolution Process

1
Initial Review:

Tournament administrators review the dispute and all provided evidence within 24 hours.

2
Investigation:

Additional information may be requested from involved parties. Match replays and server logs are analyzed.

3
Decision:

Administrators issue a final decision based on evidence and rules. All parties are notified via email.

4
Appeal (if applicable):

Appeals may be submitted within 48 hours with new evidence. Senior administrators make final ruling.

Important Notice:

Administrator decisions are considered final. DeltaCrown reserves the right to modify match results, award penalties, or take corrective action as necessary to maintain tournament integrity.
