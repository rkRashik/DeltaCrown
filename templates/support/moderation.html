Fair Play

Moderation & Fair Play

Maintaining a safe, competitive, and respectful environment for all players

Our Core Principles

The foundation of competitive integrity on DeltaCrown

Fair Competition

Every player competes on equal footing. Cheating, exploits, and unfair advantages are strictly prohibited and result in immediate action.

Respectful Community

Harassment, hate speech, and toxic behavior have no place here. We foster an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome.

Transparent Justice

All moderation actions are documented and appealable. We believe in clear communication and fair enforcement of rules.

Rules & Violations

What's not allowed on DeltaCrown

Cheating & Exploits

Using third-party software, game exploits, or any unauthorized advantage is strictly forbidden.

Permanent Ban No warnings

Match Fixing & Collusion

Intentionally losing, teaming up in solo matches, or coordinating results to manipulate rankings or prizes.

Permanent Ban All involved parties

Harassment & Toxic Behavior

Personal attacks, hate speech, discrimination, doxxing, or repeated unwanted contact.

1st: 7-day ban 2nd: 30-day ban 3rd: Permanent

Account Sharing & Boosting

Letting others play on your account or playing on someone else's to boost rankings.

14-day ban + rank reset

Smurfing (Multiple Accounts)

Creating alternate accounts to play against lower-skilled players or circumvent bans.

All accounts banned
