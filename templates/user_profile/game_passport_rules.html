1. Identity Ownership

‚úÖ ENFORCED

You may ONLY link game accounts that you personally own and have legitimate access to.

Core Principle

When you create a Game Passport, you declare under DeltaCrown's Terms of Service that you are the legitimate owner of the game account. This declaration is binding and any violation constitutes fraud.

Prohibited Activities (Zero Tolerance)

  • Account Sharing: Using credentials from another person, even with permission
  • Smurfing: Registering low-rank alternate accounts to exploit matchmaking or sandbagging
  • Boosting Services: Allowing another player to rank up your account
  • Account Trading: Buying, selling, or gifting game accounts for use on DeltaCrown

Verification Requirement

Admins may request proof of ownership at any time (screenshots, email receipts, in-game verification). Failure to provide evidence within 48 hours results in FLAGGED status and suspension.

2. Global Uniqueness Enforcement

Platform Rule: One game identity can only be linked to ONE DeltaCrown account ‚Äî forever.

Technical Implementation:

  • Database constraint enforces uniqueness on (game, identity_key)
  • Identity key is normalized and case-insensitive (e.g., "PlayerName#TAG" ‚Üí "playername:tag")
  • Attempting to link already-registered identity = immediate error

Blocked Action: "This Game ID is already linked to another account."

Why? Prevents identity theft, impersonation, and competitive fraud. If someone else registered your ID, contact support immediately.

4. Identity Lock Period ‚úÖ ENFORCED

Security Measure: Core identity fields are locked after creation to prevent smurf switching and account manipulation.

Lock duration by game:

  • Valorant, CS:GO, PUBG: 30 days
  • eFootball: 90 days (competitive season protection)
  • Other games: 30 days (default)

What's locked:

  • Primary identity fields (Steam ID, Riot ID, PUBG UID, Konami ID, etc.)
  • Account-specific identifiers marked with icon

What you CAN update:

  • Rank, tier, division (as you climb/drop)
  • Region/server (if you move or switch data centers)
  • Visibility settings (Public/Protected/Private)
  • Pinned status (showcase on profile)

Rationale: Prevents rapid identity swapping to exploit tournaments, evade bans, or manipulate rankings.

5. Passport Deletion & OTP Security

‚úÖ ENFORCED

Multi-factor verification required for all passport deletions to prevent unauthorized access.

OTP (One-Time Password) Verification

All passport deletion requests require email verification to prevent account hijacking:

  • 1. Click delete button ‚Üí System sends 6-digit code to your registered email
  • 2. Enter code within 10 minutes (expires after timeout)
  • 3. 60-second cooldown between resend requests
  • 4. 90-day cooldown applied after successful deletion (cannot re-add same game)

Deletion Restrictions (Cannot Delete If...)

  • VERIFIED Status: Admin-verified passports are permanent. Contact support for special cases.
  • Active Team Membership: Must leave team before deleting game passport.
  • Tournament Registration: Must withdraw from all active tournaments first.
  • Identity Lock Period: Cannot delete during lock window (30-90 days after creation).

90-Day Post-Deletion Cooldown

After deleting a passport, you cannot re-add that game for 90 days. This prevents:

  • ‚Ä¢ Rank manipulation by repeatedly removing/adding accounts
  • ‚Ä¢ Tournament exploitation through identity cycling
  • ‚Ä¢ Ban evasion by switching passports

Exception: Admins can override cooldown for legitimate cases (email verification change, account recovery).
