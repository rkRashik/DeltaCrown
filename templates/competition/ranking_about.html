1. Verified Match Wins

Competitive matches that have been confirmed by your opponent or verified by admins.

  • Confirmed by Opponent: 100% weight (HIGH confidence)
  • Admin Verified: 100% weight (HIGH confidence)
  • Unconfirmed with Evidence: 70% weight (MEDIUM confidence)
  • Unconfirmed without Evidence: 30% weight (LOW confidence)

2. Tournament Participation

Placement in official DeltaCrown tournaments contributes significantly to your ranking.

  • Tournament Win: Major score boost (typically 500+ points)
  • Top Placements: Scored based on position and tournament tier

üî¢ How Scores Are Calculated

Your ranking score is calculated per-game using this formula:

Total Score = (Verified Match Wins √ó Weight √ó Confidence) + Tournament Points - Decay Penalty

Example (League of Legends):

  • 10 verified match wins √ó 10 points √ó 1.0 (HIGH) = 100 points
  • 1 tournament win √ó 500 points = 500 points
  • Decay penalty (30+ days inactive) = -50 points
  • Total Score: 550 points

Per-Game vs. Global Ranking:

  • Per-Game Ranking: Your standing in a specific game (e.g., LOL, Valorant)
  • Global Ranking: Sum of all your per-game scores across all games
  • Multi-game teams can climb global leaderboards faster

‚è∞ Inactivity Decay

To keep rankings current, inactive teams experience score decay:

  • Threshold: 30 days without a match in a game
  • Decay Rate: Small penalty per day of inactivity

  • Recovery: Decay stops immediately when you resume playing
