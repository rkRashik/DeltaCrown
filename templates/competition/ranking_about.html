How Rankings Work

Crown Points (CP)

Every match you play earns or costs Crown Points. Win matches to gain CP, lose matches to lose CP. Your total CP determines your rank and tier.

Skill-Based Scaling

An invisible skill rating tracks your team's true strength. Beat a stronger team? You earn bonus CP. Lose to a weaker team? The penalty is higher.

Tier Progression

Your CP total places you into a tier. Tiers are cosmetic milestones that show your competitive level at a glance. As you earn more CP, you automatically move up.

Activity Score

Stay active to maintain your ranking. Teams that compete regularly maintain a high activity score, while inactive teams slowly decay. One match per month keeps you current.

{# ═══════════════════ FAIR PLAY ═══════════════════ #}

Fair Play Protections

Daily Match Cap: There is a limit on how many ranked matches count per day, preventing CP farming.

Opponent Diversity: Repeatedly playing the same team yields diminishing CP returns. Face new opponents for full rewards.

Activity Decay: Inactive teams gradually lose activity score, keeping the leaderboard fresh and competitive.
