Team Creation Terms & Responsibilities

1. Team Leadership & Ownership

As the team creator, you become the Team Captain with full administrative rights. You are responsible for:

  • Managing team membership (inviting, accepting, removing members)
  • Representing your team in disputes or administrative matters
  • Ensuring team compliance with DeltaCrown platform rules
  • Maintaining accurate team information and contact details

2. Team Identity & Uniqueness

  • Team names and tags must be unique across the platform
  • You may not impersonate professional teams, organizations, or brands
  • Team identities must not contain offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content
  • DeltaCrown reserves the right to rename teams that violate community standards

3. One Team Per Game Policy

To maintain competitive integrity and prevent conflicts of interest:

  • Each player may only be an active member of one team per game
  • You must leave your current team before joining another in the same game
  • Team captains cannot circumvent this by creating multiple teams
  • Violations may result in team dissolution and account restrictions

4. Competitive Conduct

Your team agrees to:

  • Compete fairly and respect opponents in all tournaments and matches
  • Follow game-specific rules and tournament regulations
  • Report any suspicious activity or rule violations
  • Accept match results and rulings from tournament administrators

5. Player Verification & Game IDs

  • All team members must verify their game IDs (Riot ID, Steam ID, etc.)
  • You are responsible for ensuring your roster uses legitimate, verified accounts
  • Using alternate accounts (smurfs) or banned accounts is prohibited
  • Teams may be disqualified if members use unverified or fraudulent IDs

6. Content & Communication

  • Team profiles, logos, banners, and descriptions must be appropriate
  • Harassment, hate speech, or abusive behavior is strictly prohibited
  • You are responsible for your team members' conduct on the platform
  • Serious or repeated violations may result in permanent team bans

7. Team Dissolution & Transfers

  • Team captains may dissolve teams at any time
  • Captain status can be transferred to another member
  • Dissolved teams cannot be restored; you must create a new team
  • Team history and stats are preserved but the roster is released

8. Prize Distribution & Payouts

For teams competing in prize tournaments:

  • The team captain is responsible for distributing prizes to members
  • DeltaCrown pays winnings to the verified team captain account
  • Internal team payment disputes are not mediated by DeltaCrown
  • Captain must provide accurate payment information to claim prizes

9. Data & Privacy

  • Team information (name, roster, match history) is public on the platform
  • We do not share personal member information without consent
  • You may request team data deletion (subject to record retention rules)
  • Match statistics and tournament results may be retained for integrity

10. Changes to Terms

  • DeltaCrown may update team policies with advance notice
  • Continued use of the team system implies acceptance of updated terms
  • Major changes will be communicated via email and platform notifications

11. Platform Rights & Enforcement

DeltaCrown reserves the right to:

  • Remove teams that violate terms or community standards
  • Restrict or ban team captains who abuse the system
  • Modify team information if necessary for compliance
  • Disqualify teams from tournaments for rule violations

12. Support & Disputes

  • For team-related issues, contact support@deltacrown.gg
  • Disputes between team members should be resolved internally first
  • We mediate only platform-related conflicts (e.g., account access)
  • Tournament disputes are handled per tournament-specific rules

By accepting these terms, you confirm:

  • You have read and understood all team creation responsibilities
  • You agree to uphold competitive integrity and fair play
  • You accept the role of Team Captain with its associated duties
  • You will comply with all DeltaCrown platform rules and policies
