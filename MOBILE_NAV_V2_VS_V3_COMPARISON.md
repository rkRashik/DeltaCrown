## 🔍 Detailed Comparison

### **1. Navigation Access**

| Aspect | V2 | V3 |
|--------|----|----|
| **Primary Menu** | Hidden in drawer | Always visible (bottom bar) |

**V2 Journey:**

2. Locate hamburger (top-left) → **0.3s**
3. Tap hamburger → **0.2s**
4. Wait for drawer animation → **0.4s**
5. Scan list for "Tournaments" → **0.8s**
6. Tap "Tournaments" → **0.2s**
